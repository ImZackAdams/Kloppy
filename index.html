What is Kloppy?

Kloppy is a cowboy paperclip-ish desktop sidekick for modern chaos in retro boots.

It wants to remember your notes, holler about reminders, watch folders like a tiny porch deputy, and pop up with advice nobody requested but everybody secretly needed.

Think utility software from a forgotten shareware CD, but with enough personality to ask why your Downloads folder contains seven files called "new-new-final-FINAL".

Why??

Because software got smooth, tasteful, and emotionally distant. Kloppy prefers pixels you can hear, buttons that look like they owe you money, and helper text with spurs.

Also because a computer should occasionally look you in the eye and say, "Partner, that file organization strategy belongs in a museum of mistakes."
